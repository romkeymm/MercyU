Methodology Notes
a. Graduates: Official count taken from SSRS Report on or after the 1st day of the subsequent term. Auto-calc uses 3-term average rate × official prior count. Toggle to override with manual estimate.
b. Retained: Taken from Assigned Registration Report. May be adjusted via attrition rate to equal official enrollment count.
c. New Students: Mid-point estimate pulled from New Student Forecast Dashboard. Includes previous students returning for additional degrees.
d. Others Returning: Students who stopped out and return to finish degree, plus correction for non-priority registration. Uses 3-term avg when no official count entered.
e. Non-Returning (plug): Calculated as residual using 3-term average non-return rate × Max to Return. Most recent 3 same-transition periods only.
